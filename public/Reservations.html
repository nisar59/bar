Reservations

Join us at Caffe Dante on Macdougal Street for dining & take away from 12 pm weekdays & 10am Saturday and Sunday, serving food until 11pm. Reservations are suggested (through Resy) and walk ins are welcomed. Reservations for dining guests only.


DANTE WEST VILLAGE

We invite you to join us for lunch & dinner at our new location, on 551 Hudson St ( corner of Perry), New York 10014.  Open daily from 10am until 1am and serving brunch from 10am on the weekends.  Reservations are suggested (through Resy) for lunch. Dinner is being served on a first come, first serve basis.
